Why Now?

The Future is Electric. In the last couple of years, we have witnessed a significant inclination to address Global concerns related to Sustainability and Climate Change. There is a need for significant work to be done to undo the damage and to save our planet. The United Arab Emirates, despite being an Oil driven economy has been heavily involved in the initiative and has actively taken measure to reduce its dependence on Oil and move towards building a more sustainable eco-system.

All active Automotive OEMs are actively transitioning to an Electric portfolio of vehicles, irrespective of class / segment of vehicles. There is a major push from the government to support the adoption of EV’s here and almost all the major automakers have announced upcoming EV models for the region from 2023 onwards. The major challenge, now, is to ensure availability of Charging locations that will enable people to Charge their vehicles. The Charging of the vehicles needs to be carried out at an easily accessible location and the entire process carried out in a seamless and convenient manner.

What We Do.

EVMERGE is a brand of RAYDN Technologies FZCO, specializing in developing and managing the EV charging infrastructure throughout the region. EVMERGE's focus is on building an extensive network of charge points that are easy to use and convenient for users. Our main focus is on bringing charging points closer to our customers. We are building a network that is convenient and accessible for both public and private users, so that Customers can easily charge their vehicles. Our user-friendly Interface makes it easy for customers to identify and charge using the charging points in a seamless manner. By making this network available, we hope to encourage all vehicle drivers to adopt more sustainable practices and transition towards e-Vehicles as an environmentally friendly option.
